Who We Are

PulseIQ Solutions is an industrial automation and systems integration company delivering reliable, right-sized control solutions for manufacturing, processing, and energy facilities worldwide.

We cover the full automation lifecycle — from conceptual design and PLC/SCADA development all the way to site commissioning, MES integration, and operator training.

Unlike large system integrators constrained by rigid processes, PulseIQ operates with agility. Every engagement starts with understanding your operational reality, not fitting you into a standard template.
