Our Story: The Pulse of Right-Sized Automation

At PULSEIQ, we believe that the most sophisticated solution isn't always the one with the most wires—it’s the one that solves the problem with the highest efficiency.

The Gap in the Industry

In the world of industrial automation, we noticed a recurring problem: a disconnect between what a client actually needs and what a supplier wants to sell.

Most providers offer a "one-size-fits-all" approach. They default to the systems they find easiest to build—which are often unnecessarily complex and costly for the end-user.

This leaves businesses trapped between two extremes: overpaying for features they will never use, or struggling with generic systems that don’t fit their unique workflow.

Why PULSEIQ was Founded

PULSEIQ was born out of a simple, disruptive idea: Automation should be an asset, not a burden.

We founded this company to bring "Intelligence" (IQ) back to the "Pulse" of your operations. We believe in right-sized technology that respects your operational reality.

Our Philosophy: Listen. Align. Automate.

A Win-Win Standard
